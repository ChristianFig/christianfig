What Do We Do?

Gain Patrol is an online fitness equipment retailer dedicated to offering quality gym products and unparalleled service. Our focus is on durable, stylish, and affordable workout gear to get you those "gains"!

Who Are We?

The Gain Patrol was formed by three college students who were inspired by fitness culture. At first, their focus was purely vanity. Then they started to see results and even found that exercising improved all aspects of their life. That's when they dedicated their lives to creating functional and attractive equipment that anyone can use.
